Glass Houses


Take sides in the epic struggle of Red vs. Blue in this futuristic map. Man the gun emplacements and rain fiery destruction on your enemies below. Find the Battle Armor to protect yourself and then escape from invaders using the anti-grav elevator tube. Finally, summon the dread mechanized Spheres of Doom to pelt the opposing team with rockets from above. Those who live in glass houses should hit the deck!
